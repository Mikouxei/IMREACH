ABOUT THE CONFERENCE
IMREACH 2024
October 10-11 | via Zoom
The 2024 URS International Multidisciplinary Research and Extension Advocacy Conference for Champions of Change (IMREACH) aims to provide a platform for researchers and extension practitioners to share knowledge, best practices, and innovations in research and community development. By fostering collaboration and disseminating significant information, the conference seeks to enhance understanding and appreciation of research and extension services as vital institutional mandates and commitments to improving lives, while also promoting partnerships and empowering communities.
Objectives:
01 Advance international research and extension collaboration.
02 Showcase impactful research and community-driven initiatives.
03 Tackle global sustainability through innovative solutions.
04 Foster interdisciplinary approaches for empowered communities.

RESEARCH PAPER REPORT FORMAT
Prepare the paper in double-spacing on A4-sized paper, using Arial font size 11 and 1-inch (25.4 mm) margins on all sides. Ensure all pages are numbered.

The required sections include the title, author(s), and an abstract of no more than 300 words. Follow with the introduction, objectives, literature review, conceptual framework, and theoretical framework.

Include a detailed methodology covering research design, participants, procedure, and data analysis. Conclude with results, discussion, recommendations, acknowledgment, and references. The paper must not exceed 30 pages.
PAPER PRESENTATION FORMAT
Only the study leader or authorized representative may present their research. Ten (10) minutes will be allocated for the pre-recorded presentation and another ten (10) minutes for the Q&A portion. Maximum of ten (10) slides only and should contain the following:
  • Title, Proponents, Duration, Budget, Implementing/Collaborating Agency
  • Introduction
  • Objectives (in bullet form)
  • Conceptual Framework
  • Methodology (by Objective)
  • Results and Discussions (in bullet form)
  • Implications and Recommendations (in bullet form)

EXTENSION PAPER REPORT FORMAT
Prepare the paper in double-spacing on A4-sized paper, using Arial font size 11 and 1-inch (25.4 mm) margins on all sides. Ensure all pages are consecutively numbered and formatted consistently for readability.

The required sections include the program/project title, faculty extensionists, and duration. Follow with the rationale, objectives, methodology, and development framework.

Include the outcomes/results of the project, conclusion, recommendation, further plan, and references.
PAPER PRESENTATION FORMAT
Only the study leader or authorized representative may present their extension project. Ten (10) minutes will be allocated for the pre-recorded presentation and another ten (10) minutes for the Q&A portion. Maximum of ten (10) slides only and the PowerPoint presentation should contain the following:
  • Program/Project Title, Faculty Extensionists, Duration, Cooperating Agency
  • Rationale (in bullet form)
  • Objectives
  • Methodology

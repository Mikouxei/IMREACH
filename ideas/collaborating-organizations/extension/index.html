FORMAT FOR EXTENSION PAPER REPORT

The paper should be typed double-spaced in A4 paper using the Arial font with a size of 11 and with a 25.4 cm margin on all sides. The paper should include the program/project title, faculty extensionists, duration, rationale, objectives, methodology, development framework, outcomes/results of the project, conclusion, recommendation, further plan, and references.

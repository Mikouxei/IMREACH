IMREACH 2024

International Multidisciplinary Research and Extension Advocacy Conference for Champions of Change

October 10-11, 2024 | Via Zoom

About the Conference

The University of Rizal System is dedicated to internationalizing its research and extension operations, aiming to make scholarly outputs and community development outcomes accessible globally. IMREACH 2024 serves as a venue for researchers and practitioners to share impactful research and best practices for empowered communities.

Conference Objectives

This objective emphasizes the importance of scaling up efforts to bring research and extension services to an international platform, enhancing global accessibility and influence.

IMREACH 2024 provides a platform for presenting qualified research and extension papers, fostering opportunities for experiential learning and practical application.

Research and extension are positioned as tools for tackling global sustainability challenges, working towards a more progressive and sustainable society.

The conference encourages collaboration across disciplines, enriching research and extension efforts with a diversity of perspectives and expertise.
